E-Scooter Safety Rules
1
Wear Protective Gear
Always wear a helmet, gloves, and appropriate footwear while riding an e-scooter for your safety.
2
Obey Traffic Laws
Follow all traffic rules and regulations, including stopping at stop signs and obeying speed limits.
3
Stay on Designated Paths
Use e-scooters only on designated paths and avoid sidewalks or pedestrian-only areas.
4
Inspect the E-Scooter
Before each ride, inspect the e-scooter for any damages or malfunctions and report them to the provider.
