How to use our app.
1
Click "Let's go"
Start by clicking the "Let's go" button to begin your journey with our app.
2
Select your model
Choose the e-scooter model that suits your needs from the available options.
3
Select location
Pick your desired location for your e-scooter ride. You can choose from various locations across the city.
4
Checkout
Review your order, make any necessary adjustments, and proceed to checkout to complete your reservation.
